Jute Rope

Use for

Jute rope is made from raw material of plant origin. It is lightweight, durable, abrasion and weather resistant. Nice to the touch, it doesn’t irritate the skin. Jute ropes are completely environmentally friendly in production and in use, therefore, they can be recycled.The use of jute rope is very broad. They are used in sailing, construction, transport, agriculture or insulation of log houses. Because of its appearance, jute rope is often used as a decorative element in homes and gardens. The secret of jute rope’s popularity lays in its properties. It is distinguished not only by its bright colours, but also strong, pleasant to the touch material of plant origin. Jute is durable, resistant to rot and environmentally friendly.
